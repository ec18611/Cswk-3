FAQs

We understand you probably have a couple of questions about what to expect when you visit us for a date with your stylist. Take a look below for answers to the most common questions we receive. If your question is not answered below just give your local salon a call and our team will be happy to help.

You can make changes to your appointment or reschedule up to 24 hours before you are due to visit. Just follow the instructions on your confirmation email and if in doubt give us a call and we will be able to help you.

We know life happens so we like to be as flexible as possible. That is why you can make changes to your appointment or reschedule up to 24 hours before you are due to visit. If you do not turn up for your appointment you will be charged a fifty percent fee of your booking as we will not have a chance to offer that appointment with your wonderful stylist to someone else.

We understand that emergencies happen. If an emergency stops you from making your appointment please just give your salon a call and our lovely team will be able to help.
